How To Choose Your Guitar

Choosing your first guitar is a big decision, it can be detrimental to your success in learning to play the guitar. Choosing the correct guitar should allow you to get the sound and feel for the music you want to learn.

There are many guitar types to choose from acoustic to hollow body electric such as the Gretsch to solid body electric. Just choosing from the solid body electric has so many options from the iconic Fender stratocaster to the legendary Gibson Les Paul. Each has it's own properties that are best for certain types of music or particular playing styles.

Just as important as the sound is comfort, you wont reach your goals as easily if it's to heavy, the neck is too wide or the frets too close. Thats what we are here for
